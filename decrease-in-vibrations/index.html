Neocork Anti-vibration Plate

Neocork is a hybrid material that combines the vibration isolating properties of cork with the shock absorbing of a balanced mix of elastomers.

The result is a material with exceptional vibration isolating properties, which can be used to solve a great number of vibration problems.

Delivering high isolation levels (70% to 95%) in most uses, depending of the frequency of the vibrating energy and the load the support receives.
