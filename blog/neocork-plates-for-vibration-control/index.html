Neocork Vibration Control Plates

01 APRIL 2022

Neocork vibration control plates have been specifically developed and designed to absorb mechanical vibrations.

Vibration Isolating Mats

They combine the properties of cork and polymers to offer the best anti-vibration isolation on the market, absorbing between 70% and 95% of vibrations.

Anti-vibration and anti-skid plates

They improve safety by eliminating vibrations that affect operators, and also prevent slips and falls, helping to create a safer work environment and increase productivity.

Supports for heavy machinery

They are suitable as a support for machinery, with a load capacity of up to 7.5 Kg/cm2. They absorb the mechanical vibrations of the machine in operation, and are also resistant to wear and to grease, oil or chemical products.

Modular Vibration Isolator

Vibration isolation plates can be cut to fit the surface to be covered. As they have a non-slip surface, they do not need glue to be anchored to the ground. This makes installation easy and allows the anti-vibration isolation plates to be removed and refitted as many times as desired.

Vibration Control

The best product on the market to eliminate vibrations. Properties and benefits:

  • Weather resistant.
  • Resistant to liquids, greases, oil and chemical products.
  • Non-slip surface.
  • No glue required for installation.
  • It can be cut into smaller pieces.

Technical characteristics

  • Material: Rubber and cork.
  • Colors: Blue and black.
  • Measurements: 24" x 36" x 1" and 24" x 18" x 1".
  • Load capacities: 3.5 Kg/cm2 and 7.5 Kg/cm2.
  • Vibration absorption capacities: between 70% and 95%.

Applications of Neocork Anti-vibration Plates

We offer a high quality anti-vibration isolator, capable of responding effectively in all types of applications:

  • Construction vibration control
  • Vibration control in the railway industry
  • Vibration control in the naval industry
  • Vibration control in mining
  • Vibration control in chemical industry
  • Vibration control in transport
  • Vibration control in heavy machinery
  • Vibration control in mechanical industry
